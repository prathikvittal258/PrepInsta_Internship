About Us

Expedia Travels is a leading online travel agency renowned for its comprehensive range of services and user-friendly platform. Founded in 1996 and subsequently spun off from Microsoft in 1999, Expedia operates globally under various brands like Expedia.com, Hotels.com, and Orbitz. It offers a one-stop solution for travelers, allowing them to book flights, hotels, vacation packages, car rentals, cruises, and activities worldwide. Serving a diverse clientele, Expedia leverages its metasearch engine to compare prices across airlines, hotels, and other providers, ensuring customers find the best deals. With a robust rewards program, Expedia Rewards, members earn points on bookings that can be redeemed for discounts and benefits. Supported by mobile apps and localized websites in multiple languages, Expedia remains a go-to platform for travelers seeking convenience, choice, and competitive pricing in their travel arrangements.
